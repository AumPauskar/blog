    Using LPC 2148 in IOT applications

    Using LPC 2148 in IOT applications The LPC2148 is a microcontroller produced by NXP Semiconductors. It’s based on a 16-bit/32-bit ARM7TDMI-S CPU with real-time emulation and embedded trace support, that combines the microcontroller with embedded high-speed flash memory. Here are some of the key hardware specifications of the LPC2148: Processor: 16-bit/32-bit ARM7TDMI-S CPU with a clock speed of up to 60 MHz. Memory: 512KB of on-chip static RAM and 32KB of on-chip flash program memory. GPIO (General Purpose Input/Output): Up to 45 5V tolerant fast GPIO pins. Communication Interfaces: Two UARTs, two I2C-bus interfaces, two SPI interfaces, and multiple 32-bit timers. ADC/DAC: 10-bit ADC and DAC. Other Features: Multiple 32-bit timers, a watchdog timer, PWM unit, real-time clock, and more. Power Supply: 3.3V operation with 5V tolerant I/O pads. Package: Available in LQFP64, LQFP48 and PLCC44 packages. The LPC2148 is suitable for a wide range of applications including industrial control, medical systems, access control, and point-of-sale. It’s particularly popular in the IoT (Internet of Things) space due to its rich peripheral set and low power consumption. ...
